Our Approach

Regal Glaging specializes in the design and installation of commercial and residential glass with deep experience in the residential, retail, hospitality, food service and industrial sectors.

Our job is to help you enhance your home or business by designing and installing glass elements to a remarkable aesthetic standard, to keep you and/or your customers and employees feeling safe and enjoying the environment.

Because our mission centers on you, our approach places you at the very center. It puts you in charge at all times, and enables you to ask questions, provide feedback, make adjustments and changes to your plans throughout the process, and it ensures at the end of that process, we will have exceeded your expectations.

  • One contract . . . and one point of contact From project conceptualization to completion, you will have a senior Regal Glazing Company Manager handling your every question, concern and decision. They, in turn, will manage all of the work performed by Regal Glazing employees and subcontractors, and be responsible for scheduling, safety plans and cost control reporting.
  • Architectural and design planning Regal Glazing Glazing, through its sister company, Regal Glazing Design, can provide in-house architectural and interior design support directly, or will work closely with an architectural or design team of your choosing. Either way, you will receive multiple design ideas that accommodate your requirements and budget.
  • Construction and modifications As your prime contractor, Regal Glazing can oversee and coordinate our work as one integrated team to ensure the project meets your and our exacting standards, and to ensure that your work is completed as quickly and cost-effectively as possible.
